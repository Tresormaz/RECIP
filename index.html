Chardonnay-Deli

An upmarket farm stall that serves light meals, set in the beautiful Constantia Valley. Brought to you by Charmaine Lilje who owned the successful catering company Chardonnay Chefs, this is where you will be able to find a variety of healthy and homemade products. Chardonnay Deli has a small organic range, a variety of gluten free products, fresh produce and a range of free range grass fed meats. Chardonnay Deli offer delicious meals which change daily, all served to you in a relaxed, stylish venue that is steeped in history.
